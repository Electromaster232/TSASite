CloudComputing

There are two kinds of cloud computing, Cloud Storage and Cloud Servers.


  1. Cloud Storage:

A service that lets you upload any documents, pictures, or other information, to a server, meaning, it is not stored on your computer, and you can access it from anywhere. It is entirely private and generally requires an account.

 

Cloud Servers:

A service that allows you to create a “Virtual Computer” on a server, allowing you to for example host a website, or store information, all secured and only accessible to you.
